|Bank | Number of digits | Example |
|-----|------------------|---------|
|FNB | 11 digits | 50050221924 |
|Absa| 10 digits | 4074262420 |
|Capitec | 10 digits | 1417824741 |
|Nedbank | 10 to 12 digits| |
